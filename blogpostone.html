My first blog

This is my first blog! And its a story about this website, today on 12/29/2023..



What happened?

It all started, when i opened my 2nd issue on Github. About how the HTML file name kept replacing the repo name.

I spent, 4-5 hours looking for a solution to my code, what could possibly be the problem? I had been looking for so long, it had to be something, right??

It was, in fact something i was overlooking.

I had just now figured out, the whole reason why it wasn't working, the whole reason WHY it had never worked, was because of....



A SLASH.

That is right. It was all because of a slash, all i had to do to fix this entire thing, was to delete a slash. That was it. A SLASH.



Now, i know this is a weird first blog, but i decided to share it with you because.. Well, it was funny, but really sad. But anyway, thank you for reading, and have a great rest of your day!
