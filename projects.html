Tech independence Journey, a project i co-founded with a person about a year ago. Now discontinued, but i will recontinue it in the future.

What is it?

Tech Independence Journey is a website all about taking back your privacy from big tech, whether that would be Google, Facebook, or about any company in the big tech industry. You can find alternatives to their products, for example an privacy-respecting alternative to Google Chrome.
